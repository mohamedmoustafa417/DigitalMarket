Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective date: 18 May 2026 · Last updated: 18 May 2026

This Refund & Cancellation Policy explains when and how you can request a refund for purchases made on DigitalMarket (digitalmarketstore.shop), an Egyptian marketplace for licensed digital products and educational content. It complies with Egyptian Consumer Protection Law (Law No. 181 of 2018) and the requirements of our payment processors.

1. Summary

Digital goods are non-returnable in the traditional sense — once the download link is delivered, the file can't be "returned." However, DigitalMarket honors refund requests in specific situations listed below. You must request a refund within 7 days of purchase.

2. Eligible refund reasons

You are entitled to a full refund if any of the following apply:

  • Product not delivered — your order was approved but the download links never appeared or all of them are broken.
  • Product materially different from description — the file format, content, language, version, or deliverables differ significantly from what the seller advertised.
  • File corruption / unusable file — the downloaded file is corrupted, password-locked without the password, or cannot be opened with the software listed in the product description.
  • Duplicate charge — you were charged more than once for the same order.
  • Unauthorized purchase — your account or payment method was used without your permission. (Police report or proof of identity theft may be required.)
  • Copyright violation — the seller listed pirated, cracked, or copyright-infringing material. You also get a refund if a DMCA takedown affected your file.

3. Non-eligible reasons

Refunds will not be issued for:

  • "I changed my mind" / buyer's remorse;
  • "I already downloaded the file and don't need it anymore";
  • "I didn't like the content" — this is subjective and downloads aren't reversible;
  • Compatibility issues you could have foreseen (e.g. buying a .PSD without owning Photoshop);
  • Requests made more than 7 days after the order was approved;
  • Any purchase of a product violating our Acceptable Use Policy (e.g. SMM panels, pirated software) — these orders are voided without refund.

4. How to request a refund

  1. Sign in to your DigitalMarket account.
  2. Go to My Orders from the profile menu.
  3. Find the order, click Request Refund, choose a reason, and describe the problem.
  4. Or email support@digitalmarketstore.shop with your order number (#XXXXXXXX) and a description of the issue.

6. Cancellation before approval

Orders that are still in "pending" status (i.e. before an admin has reviewed your payment proof and approved the order) can be cancelled instantly with a full refund. Use the Cancel Order button on the My Orders page, or email us. Once an order is marked "approved" and the download token is issued, the standard refund policy (sections 2–5) applies.

7. Method of refund

Refunds are issued to the original payment method used for the order. Bank-transfer and Instapay refunds usually appear within 5–10 business days. Card refunds (via Kashier or other processors) post to the card statement within the issuer's processing window — typically 7–14 business days.

Egyptian banks & weekends: refund processing time does not include Fridays, Saturdays, or Egyptian public holidays.

8. Disputed refunds (chargebacks)

Before filing a chargeback with your bank or card issuer, please give our team a chance to resolve the issue — we can almost always issue a refund faster than a chargeback. Filing a chargeback while a refund request is open will result in your account being suspended pending review. Fraudulent chargebacks are reported to the Egyptian Anti-Cyber Crime Unit.

9. Seller-initiated refunds

Sellers can issue full or partial refunds to buyers at their discretion (e.g. if they realize their product had a defect). DigitalMarket will process the refund from the seller's pending balance.
